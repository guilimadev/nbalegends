Scouting Report
He really excels on the offensive end of the floor with his scoring ability... Has a very nice back to the basket game, very crafty... Great at drawing fouls and converting from the free throw line... Has excellent vision and passing ability for a player his size... Scrappy rebounder who picks up about 5 or 6 on a consistent basis... Long arms and quick hands make him a defensive nightmare... Great lateral speed on defense; good reflexes as well as leaping ability make him a good shot blocker... Shows the ability to raise his level of play in big games... Center with great size and strength
