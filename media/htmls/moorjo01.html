Scouting Report
Jump shot has improved... Gets to the line well and shoots at a solid clip... He has the uncanny ability to see passing lanes before they open up, thus enabling him to nearly always make the right decision with the ball... Has a great fake and hesitations package, which he uses to beat his defender off the dribble or on fast-break.... Excellent foot speed, overall quickness and effort makes him a tough defensive player... Has a good feel for the game

He has problems defending stronger guys and playing post up on offense, has to improve his timing on rebounds and his patience on defense.
