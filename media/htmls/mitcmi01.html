Scouting Report
Excellent scorer with good mental toughness... Has improved his free throw shooting substantially... Shows solid ball handling ability but needs to cut down on turnovers... Has the versatility to guard on the perimeter and in the post... He knows how to take what the defense gives him, knows when to pull up, when to drive and when to pass ...... Lacks a great shot from outside, scores mostly within 12 feet of the basket... Lacks the ability to improvise much in the open floor: catching and dribbling/passing before a shot, he's a lot more comfortable with set plays or crashing the glass
