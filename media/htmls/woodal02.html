Scouting Report
His mechanics are solid and has good form and knows when to pull up... Free throw shooting showed significant improvement last year... Plays sound defense and his foot speed can create problems for opponents... Effective thief

Lacks great range and consistency on his outside shot... His ability to fill in as a point is limited due to his poor vision and passing... He has problems defending stronger guys and playing post up on offense, has to improve his timing on rebounds and his patience on defense.... Has some innate understanding of the game but increased situational awareness will take his play to new heights
