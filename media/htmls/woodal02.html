Scouting Report
He is adept at creating shots for himself off the dribble... Solid free throw shooter... Decent passer who can handle pressure and pass out of a double team... Attacks the basket well with good ball handling ability... Has the versatility to guard on the perimeter and in the post... Effective thief... Never appears tired, has great conditioning and work ethic

Needs to improve upon his outside shot, which is solid but can improve... Often jumps straight up for rebounds instead of boxing out... Does not get great lift when he jumps... His feel for the game is still improving.
