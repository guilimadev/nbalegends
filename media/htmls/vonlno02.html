Scouting Report
He plays one-on-one very well, has nice moves around the basket where he usually finishes with power plays.... Gets to the line well and shoots at a solid clip... Solid ball handler, doesn't over dribble... Loves using his hops to attack the basket... Long arms help out with closing passing lanes and getting to the rim... He is not too aggressive but not afraid either... Physically overpowering type of player

Can be prone to shooting slumps, a streaky shooter, his field goal percentage dipped considerably without a quality point guard setting him up... Passing skills and vision are decent... Has decent shot blocking skills due to his size, but lacks great quickness and reflexes vertically
