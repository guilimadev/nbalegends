Scouting Report
He understands how to create shots for himself and has incredible leaping ability... Hard worker, his outside shot has shown steady improvement... Quality free throw shooter... He has a killer crossover that he uses to get by his man on the perimeter and is athletic, getting good lift on his jumper... Defensively he does a good job staying in front of his man and limiting drives... Competitive, good decision maker... Never appears tired, has great conditioning and work ethic

Leaping ability is average... Average size and athleticism makes him an average defender, but he gives solid effort
