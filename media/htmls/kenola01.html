Excellent body control going up for ally oops and going to the basket... Good free throw shooter

Still learning the nuances, with timing and moving without the ball, and freeing himself for shots... Lacks the ability to improvise much in the open floor: catching and dribbling/passing before a shot, he's a lot more comfortable with set plays or crashing the glass... He has problems defending stronger guys and playing post up on offense, has to improve his timing on rebounds and his patience on defense.
