Scouting Report
Can create his own shot almost at will... Good free throw shooter, but should look to get to the line more often... Good basketball disposition, doesn't get out of control or frustrated easily, makes good decisions passing the ball and has good vision... Solid rebounder... He can defend bigger players and quicker ones... Anticipates passes well and forces a ton of steals with his on ball pressure... Unlike many players as athletic as he is, he doesn't rely on it he uses intelligence and skills instead... An absolutely jaw dropping physical specimen with his combination of size, length, strength, athleticism and mobility ...... His shot from outside needs major work, though it showed improvement
