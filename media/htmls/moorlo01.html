Scouting Report
A decent outside shooter, also scores well on the move with a nice repertoire of mid range shots... Gets to the line well and shoots at a solid clip... Creates well off the dribble with a quick first step... Has a great fake and hesitations package, which he uses to beat his defender off the dribble or on fast-break.... Excellent at anticipating passes, ball hawking style

As a shooter, he doesn’t elevate all that well on his jumper and is content with being a flat footed set shooter... Often goes for the flashy play instead of the simple, smart, play. Also could use some work on defense, mostly staying fundamental and exerting the same intensity and effort
