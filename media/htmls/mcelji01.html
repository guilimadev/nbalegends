Scouting Report
Aggressive player who gets to the line often and converts his FTs at a good clip... Owns a variety of offense weapons, but is at his best when breaking down defenders with his lethal crossover, or feeding a teammate for an easy basket ...... In these situations is where he shows his great scoring ability, but he has a tendency to force shots and make poor decisions... Has bad court vision and no real passing ability... Aggressiveness defensively can lead to foul problems, he can also get caught gambling for steals
